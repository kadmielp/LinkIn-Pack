How to export bookmarks

Export bookmarks as an HTML file, then open that file here. Browser internal links may need to be copied into the address bar if your browser blocks direct navigation from this page.

Chrome

  1. Open chrome://bookmarks/ or press Ctrl+Shift+O.
  2. Open the three-dot menu.
  3. Choose Export bookmarks.
chrome://bookmarks/

Edge

  1. Open edge://favorites/ or press Ctrl+Shift+O.
  2. Open the three-dot menu.
  3. Choose Export favorites.
edge://favorites/

Firefox

  1. Press Ctrl+Shift+O to open Library.
  2. Choose Import and Backup.
  3. Choose Export Bookmarks to HTML.

Brave

  1. Open brave://bookmarks/ or press Ctrl+Shift+O.
  2. Open the three-dot menu.
  3. Choose Export bookmarks.
brave://bookmarks/

Comet

  1. Open comet://bookmarks/.
  2. Open the three-dot menu.
  3. Choose Export bookmarks.
comet://bookmarks/

Opera

  1. Open opera://bookmarks/ or press Ctrl+Shift+B.
  2. Open the bookmark manager menu.
  3. Choose Export bookmarks.
opera://bookmarks/

Safari

  1. Open Safari.
  2. Choose File, then Export.
  3. Choose Bookmarks.
Open an exported bookmarks HTML file to begin.
